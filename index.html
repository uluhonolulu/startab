Set your startup's goals and watch performance daily

If you're a solo founder like me, you have so much to do during the day. It's so easy to get lost in the details and forget to take a look at the big picture.
StarTab is a browser extension that lets you start your day with seeing how you are doing with your startup. You will see your main analytic reports and how you approach your goals, all in your browser's New tab or Home page. Pull data from Google Analytics, Stripe, or whatever reporting tool you use and compare with your set goals. Get an embed code to show your metrics to other founders and become a part of the Open Startup movement.
